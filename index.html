Welcome to LAZURE Apartment Nikiti

Your private, elegant retreat in the heart of Sithonia.

This cozy 25 m² one-room apartment is located on the ground floor of the Bomo Nikiti complex , just 700 m from the beach and promenade. With a private entrance, modern amenities, and a quiet garden setting, it’s ideal for couples or small families looking for comfort and convenience. It is ideal for discovering the natural beauty of the Sithonia peninsula, with its crystal-clear Blue Flag beaches , as well as the breathtaking landscapes of Halkidiki, Macedonia.
